About Integer Group SA

Integer Group is a financial services product provider, established in 2013, offering investment opportunities, online software products, and 1-on-1 coaching.

FOREX Trading

We introduce you to online FOREX trading, a global decentralized market trading currencies, indices, and commodities, where countries compete 24/7. We assist investors with opening trading accounts, offering insights and advice for successful trades.

Partnerships with Brokers

We partner with local and international brokers to provide you with access to leading tech platforms, ensuring successful trades and maximizing profits through sound financial strategies.

Software Programs

Our software programs, such as Plus 500, are designed for individuals who want to learn how to trade online, providing the necessary tools and strategies for successful trading.

Client Communication

Our communication channels ensure that clients receive 24/7 support from our skilled in-house consultants and online accounts analysts, who provide accurate information and trading advice.
